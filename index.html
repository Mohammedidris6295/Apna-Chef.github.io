Hyderabadi Chicken Dum Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 500 g Basmati Rice
  • 200 g Yogurt
  • 3 Onions, sliced
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander and Mint Leaves
  • 4 Green Chilies
  • 2 Lemons
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • Whole spices (Cinnamon, Cloves, Bay leaves, Cardamom)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it sit for at least 1 hour.
  2. Fry onions in ghee until golden brown and set aside.
  3. Cook the marinated chicken until it's half cooked. Add tomatoes, green chilies, coriander, and mint leaves.
  4. In another pot, boil rice with whole spices until it's 70% cooked.
  5. Layer the half-cooked chicken and rice in a pot. Sprinkle fried onions, lemon juice, and ghee between layers.
  6. Seal the pot with dough or a tight lid and cook on low flame (dum) for 30-40 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Nutritional Value (per serving)

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbohydrates: 55g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sugar: 5g
  • Sodium: 600mg
